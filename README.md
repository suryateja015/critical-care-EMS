Critical Care Emergency Medical Services (CCEMS) is a specialized component of emergency medical services that focuses on providing advanced pre-hospital care for patients with life-threatening conditions requiring immediate intervention. CCEMS teams, often comprised of paramedics, critical care nurses and physicians, are equipped with advanced medical technologies and medications to manage complex cases such as severe trauma, cardiac arrest, respiratory failure, and neurological emergencies. The goal of CCEMS is to stabilize patients during transport to specialized healthcare facilities, improving outcomes and minimising the risk of morbidity and mortality. This system involves in reducing the death rate in the case of heavy traffics. This system provides an interface between the patient and their required hospitals according to their specializations. This project can be implemented by developing a separate coach in metro trains with proper medical equipments (Ambulance).
